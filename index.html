Personal Portfolio

Hi! My name is Joshua Haug and I am a graphic designer based in Northern Virginia. I’m a creative and dedicated content developer with experience in social media advertising and auditing, as well as advertisement styling and design. I enjoy getting to work with others to create dynamic ad campaigns for websites and social media platforms.

Study Abroad

 During the summer of 2023, I participated in “JMU Ireland: Media Culture, and Society” where I got to work with other students to create and design a multimedia website. I designed the logo, font style, and color scheme for the site.

Logo Design

Along with other students, I designed a logo for our collaborative website. We went with a green color scheme to match the Ireland asthetic and used our title "Galway: Tales and Terrors" to create this logo." 

Social Media Marketing

Through several SMAD classes, I have created several social media ad campaigns. I've also had the opportunity to be the social media manager for various accounts. Here's a look at some of the social media content I have created.

Adagio House

As part of a SMAD class, I created several pieces of social media content for Adagio House, a mental health clinic in Harrisonburg, VA. Before I created the content, I conducted a social media audit across several of their platforms. Along with other students, I created a posting schedule and created content that was used by Adagio House.

Reel Change Film Festival

I got to create several social media ads for the Reel Change Film Festival, which is a JMU-ran film festival that showcases films created by JMU students. I also created these ads for print and digital.

Ad Campaigns

As part of a SMAD class, I got to design an ad campaign for Piero's, an Italian restaurant in Chantilly, VA. I designed menus, business cards, and fliers. I also created a color scheme and font style for these advertisements. 

Ole School Alumni Group

As part of a class, I organized a multimedia ad campaign for the Ole School Alumni Group, a non-profit organization that encourages enrollment of minority students at JMU by providing scholarships and recruitment opportunities for students of color. I created print, social, and digital ads for the group.
